About Developers

This page tells us about the people who have contributed towards the development and maintaining of this website.

Late Sh. Suresh Chandra Gupta

Sh. Suresh Chandra Gupta is the founder of this Website.
He originally comes up with the idea of creating one portal where all information related to Mathur Vaishya community will be displayed so that it will be accessible to everyone.
He released the first major version of this website in year 2011, and Since then he was maintaining this website.
He had completed his Bachelor in Engineering from Madhav Engineering College, Jiwaji University, Gwalior in year 1969.
He worked in Bharat Sanchar Nigam Limited for around 34 years.
During his work tenure he went to Oklahoma State University, USA for Opto Electronics training under UNDP Project.
He lived in Ghaziabad with his Wife, two Daughters and one Son
He retired from there in year 2005 as Divisional Engineer from Advanced Level Telecom Training Centre, Ghaziabad
Unfortunately today he is no more between us because of his sad demise in the month of February 2020.
